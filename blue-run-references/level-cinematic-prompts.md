# Blue Run — Level-Intro Cinematic Prompts
For Grok / Runway / any text-to-video model. Target: ~4-5 seconds per clip, 16:9 landscape, no text or watermarks in frame (title cards are added by the game itself).

## Global style block (prepend to EVERY prompt for consistency)
> Stylized 3D animated underwater world in the style of a premium Pixar adventure film. Saturated teal and gold color palette, soft rounded shapes, volumetric god rays piercing down through water, drifting bubbles and plankton motes, gentle caustic light patterns dancing on surfaces. Cinematic depth of field, anamorphic lens, high production value animation, smooth dolly camera. No text, no watermark, no humans.

---

## Level 0 — CALM REEFS
Camera glides forward low over a sunlit sandy canyon floor between two coral-covered rock walls. Warm amber sunlight from above blends into bright turquoise water. Pink, orange and yellow coral fans sway gently; small fish schools scatter as the camera passes. A tiny cheerful blue cartoon fish with big eyes darts ahead of the camera into the distance, leaving a sparkling bubble trail. Mood: joyful, inviting, morning-light adventure.

## Level 1 — STORMY CURRENTS
The water darkens to steel blue-grey. Camera pushes forward through churning currents between jagged canyon walls, kelp whipping sideways in the surge. Shafts of pale storm light flicker from above. Debris and sand swirl past the lens. In the distance, a green moray eel uncoils from a crevice and snaps at the camera as it rushes past. Mood: urgent, wind-tunnel intensity, the calm is over.

## Level 2 — ANCIENT ABYSS
Near-total darkness. The camera descends slowly into a black abyss where bioluminescent purple and cyan coral pulse like heartbeats along ancient rock spires. Ghostly jellyfish drift up past the lens, glowing softly. Far below in the murk, an impossibly huge dark silhouette with two faint yellow eyes glides across the frame and vanishes. A deep sub-bass rumble seems to shake the water. Mood: dread, wonder, something ancient has noticed you.

## Level 3 — TREASURE PALACE
Purple twilight water. The camera sweeps up over a ruined sunken temple of golden columns half-buried in sand, treasure chests spilling gold coins that glint in shafts of violet light. Pearls and gold coins float suspended like stars. Suddenly a colossal dark tentacle covered in glowing purple suckers wraps around a temple column in the background and crushes it to rubble. Mood: greed and glory turning to danger — grab the treasure and RUN.

## Level 4 — BOSS ESCAPE
Blood-red water, almost boiling. The camera races forward at breakneck speed through a collapsing canyon as enormous black tentacles with glowing purple suckers smash down from above, pulverizing rock on both sides. Shockwaves ripple through the water. Behind the camera, two massive yellow slit eyes ignite in the crimson murk, closing in. The canyon ahead narrows to a single shaft of golden light. Mood: pure escape adrenaline, the final chase, swim or die.

## Bonus — Title screen loop (10s, seamless loop)
Slow ambient drift over the sunlit calm reef from Level 0, god rays sweeping gently, bubbles rising, coral swaying, small fish circling. No camera destination — a living painting. Loopable: first and last frame match. Mood: serene invitation.

## Generation settings tips
- Duration: 4-5s per level clip (game shows them once per level transition, tap-to-skip)
- Resolution: 1280x720 is plenty (fullscreen video overlay, compressed for web)
- Aspect: 16:9
- If the model supports a style reference image: screenshot the actual game and attach it for palette matching
- Ask for "smooth constant camera speed, no cuts" — single-shot clips feel more premium than edited ones
- File naming for the game integration: lv0.mp4, lv1.mp4, lv2.mp4, lv3.mp4, lv4.mp4, title.mp4
